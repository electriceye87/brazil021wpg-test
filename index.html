Adults & Teens Program

BJJ - Fundamentals

An on-going jiu-jitsu class for teens and adults that includes self-defense and basic BJJ skills such as takedowns, guard passing, positional control and basic submissions. There will be sparring at the end of every class and semi-annual belt promotions.

BJJ - Advanced

Advanced jiu-jitsu techniques largely focused on sparring, open to all members but techniques will be more complicated than fundamentals class and include open guard sweeps, advanced submissions, detailed guard passes.
