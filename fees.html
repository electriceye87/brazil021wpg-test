Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Gi for my first class? Do I need anything else?

No! For your free trial, comfortable workout clothes like a t-shirt and shorts are perfect. If you decide to join, we can help you select the right Gi. As well, please make sure you sign up at this link.

Are there any long-term contracts?

We believe in the quality of our instruction. All our memberships are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required.

Are classes seasonal?

Generally, our classes run year round. On occasion, classes may be cancelled, so please contact us for the most current information.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We do offer slight discounts for cash end E-Transfer payments. Please contact us for more information.
